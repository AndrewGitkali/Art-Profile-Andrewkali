About

I am Andrew Kali, a professional illustrator based in Zambia, specializing in comic book art, book covers, children's literature, and novel illustrations. With a foundation in traditional techniques and expertise in digital artistry, I deliver exceptional visual narratives that captivate audiences and elevate storytelling.

My commitment to excellence and attention to detail ensures that every project receives the professional treatment it deserves, combining artistic vision with commercial viability.
